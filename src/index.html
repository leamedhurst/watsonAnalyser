Watson Qualitative Analyzer

Natural language processing for advanced text analysis

Alpha

Watson Qualitative Analyzer uses the Watson Alchemy API which is a collection of natural language processing APIs that help you understand sentiment, keywords, entities, high-level concepts and more. You can use AlchemyLanguage to understand how your qualitative data and how your employees feel about your organisation using the natural language of your employees, to automatically classify the contents of comments, or to see what topics are trending in your surveys. AlchemyLanguage provides many pre-trained features, but you can also create a custom model using Watson Knowledge Studio.
